Behind the singles

Songs that didn't need an album.

Some songs arrive whole. They don't belong to a record, don't need a sequence around them — they just need to exist. These four singles are those moments: snapshots from quieter chapters, written between bigger projects, each one a small contained world.

Whisper is about listening. One Day is about wanting. Renegade is about leaving. Free Man is about finally arriving. Together they cover the distance between trying to escape and actually being free.
