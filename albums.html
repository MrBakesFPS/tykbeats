Behind The Music — Albums

Broken Promises

Clueless

Clueless was created for those who had that one true love that got away. The person that was only ever good to them, regardless of all the stupid decisions that they made. Even the choices that others never knew about yet was the reason that pushed them away.

Spaced Out

Spaced Out is about being lost in space and time, created during one of the most difficult parts of my life. I was a lost soul during that time, trying to find my way back in any way possible, but never could find a way to fully recover.

Dreamin

Dreamin is about looking back on all the mistakes I've made in the past, thinking about all the ways that I could've been different and all the things that I could've done better.

Kitten Shaped Pancakes

Kitten Shaped Pancakes was originally written for somebody that I fell hard for, thinking that they would've been the love of my life. I had never met them in person, but we spent an extraordinary amount of time together regardless.

Take Off!

Take Off! is all about new beginnings, starting over and starting fresh. The past is in the past, you just have to keep moving forward with strength, motivation and dedication. Who knows where life will take you!

Was A King

Was A King is all about reflecting on how I thought I was the coolest guy ever right out of high school. I had my own place, got engaged, and started making a ton of mistakes, thinking I was truly invincible. little did I know how wrong I really was.

Powfu (Miss You)

Powfu (Miss You) was created for the one that got away, and how Powfu rap takes me back to the days when I was still with them, as well as all the emotions and feelings behind the days where I was still missing them too.

Starry Skies

Starry Skies is for the lost and lonely souls who are trying to find their purpose, or trying to find the love of their life. Everyone on this Earth has a purpose, you just have to find it.

Raindrops

Raindrops is about how sometimes, love can feel too good to be true. Even if you have your doubts, and your struggles, there will always be a way to make it back. Even if the past still haunts you, as long as you keep moving forward, the right one will come along or even come back.

Lost Boy Found

Lost Boy Found is a letter written to a former love, letting them know how much better of a person I've become in comparison to who I was before, and how they had helped me grow and learn from my mistakes. They were one of the biggest reasons for me to seek out change in my life, and for that I am truly grateful.
