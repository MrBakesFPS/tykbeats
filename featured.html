Behind this release

The one about listening.

Whisper is about being in the moment and taking in your surroundings. Hearing the world around you — the birds chirp, the hum of the wind — and listening as those pesky whispers in your head start to fade away.

Some songs are loud. This one isn't. It's a song meant for a slow morning, a long drive, a quiet hour where you finally let yourself just be.
